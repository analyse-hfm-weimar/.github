## Project Overview

The goal of CAMAT is to provide a **comprehensive, modular, and reproducible environment** for the computational analysis of symbolic music data. The toolbox is designed to support both **exploratory and large-scale analytical workflows** in musicology, music theory, and music pedagogy.

CAMAT integrates established tools and standards for symbolic music representation and analysis, combining:

* **Interactive score rendering**
* **Annotation and visualization**
* **Statistical and corpus-based analysis**
* **Pattern-search methods** for melodic, harmonic, rhythmic, and textural features

A strong emphasis is placed on **open data**, **open workflows**, and **reproducibility** via Jupyter-based research environments.
